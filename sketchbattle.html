☰ Sketch Battles

A flower does not think of competing to the flower next to it.It just blooms. Similarly, artworks cannot be compared, they just flourish in their own way. So, come and see them flourish in the Battles.

Visit Our Instagram Page and Enjoy the Sketch Battles in the Highlights


 We are going to have 15 Battles, one every day.
 Each participant will submit their artwork on our website and the first 30 participants will be put into the battles.
 Participation will be free of cost. Submissions Start from 30th May. Submissions will be closed once first 30 artworks get registered.
 The Battles will be organized using the Instagram Stories' Poll, and each battle will have a winner.
 At last, the 15 Winners will be compared to find out the Top 3 Winners, who will be featured on our Instagram Page and Website.
