About Us

We are a bunch of enthusiastic young minds who believe that every person inherits a world of his own dreams in his mind. Art is present everywhere and in many forms. But it remains in solidarity for a long time due to absence of a proper platform. So here we are determined to give everyone a space to sketch out your thoughts, ideas, feelings or anything you desire.
